Privacy Policy

WP Tools — Wear Parts & Equipment Co.

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Overview

WP Tools is a private utility application developed for use by authorized field representatives of Wear Parts & Equipment Co. The app is distributed as an unlisted App Store app and is not publicly searchable. Access is gated by a server-side whitelist — only provisioned Apple IDs can sign in.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with your Apple ID, we receive a stable Apple user identifier and, if you choose to share them, your name and email address. This information is used solely to authenticate your account and confirm you are on the authorized access list. Email is stored for reference only and is never used as a login credential.

Setup Sheets and Field Notes: Blade configuration summaries, measurements, notes, and photos you create in the app are stored locally on your device. They are not transmitted to or stored on Wear Parts & Equipment Co. servers. Your setup sheet history stays on your phone.

Parts Catalog: The app downloads an updated parts catalog database from Wear Parts & Equipment Co. servers when a new version is available. This download is one-way and anonymous — no information about you or your usage is sent as part of this process.

Diagnostic and Error Telemetry: The app may report diagnostic events to our servers, including app errors and network failures. This data is used solely to identify and fix issues. No personal information, setup sheet content, or measurement data is included in these events.

Data We Do Not Collect

WP Tools does not collect location data, voice or audio data, usage analytics, or any information about how you use the app's features. There are no third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies in this app.

How We Use Your Data

Account information (Apple user identifier, name, email) is used exclusively to authenticate you and verify your access to the app. It is not used for marketing, reporting, or shared with any third party.

Data Storage and Security

Setup sheets, photos, and saved configurations are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area and are covered by your device's standard iCloud Backup if enabled. Account credentials (authentication tokens) are stored in the iOS Keychain. Data transmitted between the app and our servers uses HTTPS.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from within the app by going to Settings and selecting Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your Apple user identifier, name, and email from our servers and revokes your access. Local data (setup sheets, photos, saved configurations) is also cleared from your device at the time of deletion. To request manual data deletion or for any data-related inquiry, contact us at the address below.

Data Retention

Account information is retained for as long as your account is active. If your account is deactivated by an administrator, your information is retained in a deactivated state and access is revoked. You may request deletion at any time via the in-app Delete Account option or by contacting us directly.
