Privacy Policy

WP Field — Wear Parts & Equipment Co.

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Overview

WP Field is a private enterprise application developed by Wear Parts & Equipment Co. for internal use by its sales team. The app is distributed exclusively through Apple Business Manager and is not available on the public App Store.

Data We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with your Apple ID, we receive your name and email address. This is used to identify your account and associate your activity within the app.

Location Data: When you use GPS check-in, the app collects your precise location to record where a customer visit took place. Location data is only collected when you actively initiate a GPS check-in or when CarPlay is active and used to suggest nearby customers. The app does not track your location in the background.

Call Report Content: Notes, customer selections, call outcomes, and follow-up items you enter into the app are stored as part of your call reports.

Voice and Speech Recognition: When you use voice dictation to enter call report notes, your spoken input is processed by Apple's on-device Speech Recognition framework. Audio is transcribed locally on your device and is not sent to Wear Parts & Equipment Co. servers. The transcribed text is stored as part of your call report notes.

On-Device Text Processing (Apple Foundation Models): On supported devices (iPhone 15 Pro and later, running iOS 26 or later), WP Field uses Apple's Foundation Models framework to clean grammar and punctuation in voice-dictated call report notes. This processing happens entirely on your device — your dictation content is never transmitted to Apple, to Wear Parts & Equipment Co. servers, or to any third party. The app records reliability metrics (event type, processing duration, length of text processed, and outcome) to monitor feature performance; these metrics do not include the content of your dictation.

iOS Writing Tools (Apple Intelligence): On devices that support Apple Intelligence (iPhone 15 Pro and later, iOS 18 or later), iOS provides a system-level Writing Tools feature in all text input fields — including call report notes, contact notes, and broadcast messages. If you select text in one of these fields and choose an option such as Rewrite, Proofread, or Summarize, iOS may send the selected text to Apple's Private Cloud Compute to complete the operation. Writing Tools is a feature of iOS, not of WP Field; Wear Parts & Equipment Co. does not control, receive, or retain any data processed through Writing Tools. Apple states that data sent to Private Cloud Compute is used only to fulfill the immediate request, is not stored or retained by Apple, and is not used to train Apple models. If you prefer not to use Writing Tools in a field, you can disable it by long-pressing within the input field and selecting "Disable Writing Tools" from the context menu. For Apple's full privacy policy for Apple Intelligence, see apple.com/legal/privacy.

Diagnostic and Error Telemetry: The app reports diagnostic events to our servers, including app errors, sync failures, and permission status. This data is used solely to identify and fix issues. No personal information or report content is included in these events.

CarPlay Integration: When you use WP Field with CarPlay in a supported vehicle, the app displays a limited interface on your vehicle's built-in screen showing your assigned customers, recent reports, and follow-ups. CarPlay does not collect any new categories of data — it only displays existing app data through your vehicle's interface. The vehicle itself does not store this data; it is rendered on-screen by your iPhone.

Siri and App Intents: WP Field supports Siri voice commands and Apple App Intents, allowing you to log call reports by speaking to Siri directly (e.g., "Log a report in WP Field") or through CarPlay. When you use these features, your spoken input is processed by Apple's Speech Recognition and SiriKit frameworks using the same on-device transcription as in-app dictation. The transcribed text is stored as part of your call report.

Administrative Broadcast Messages: When your sales administrator sends a broadcast message to all reps, the message content (title and body) is stored on WP Field's servers for delivery and is retained for the message's expiry period (typically 24 hours or as configured by your administrator). Recalled messages remain in the server record with a recall timestamp but are no longer delivered. These messages contain no personally identifying information about you and are visible only to authenticated users of your organization's WP Field deployment.

How We Use Your Data

All data collected is used exclusively for internal business operations at Wear Parts & Equipment Co., including recording and reviewing sales call activity, tracking customer visit locations, and managing sales follow-ups and reporting.

Data Storage and Security

Your data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored on secure servers operated by Wear Parts & Equipment Co. Data is not sold, shared with, or disclosed to any third parties.

No Third-Party Analytics or Advertising

WP Field does not use any third-party analytics SDKs, advertising frameworks, or tracking technologies.

Background Activity

WP Field may sync data in the background when Background App Refresh is enabled on your device. Background sync is used only to keep your customer list and report history up to date. No location data is collected during background sync.

CarPlay Use

WP Field supports CarPlay so sales representatives can safely access customer information, log visits, and review recent activity while driving between customer locations. CarPlay use is optional and can be enabled or disabled per user.

When CarPlay is active:

  • The app displays a simplified interface optimized for in-vehicle use
  • Location data may be used to suggest nearby customers
  • Voice commands via Siri can be used to log quick check-ins
  • The same data security practices described above apply to all CarPlay activity

Your iPhone remains the source of all data. The vehicle's CarPlay interface is a display-only surface — no app data is stored on the vehicle.

Notifications

If you grant notification permission, WP Field sends local reminders for upcoming and overdue follow-ups. Notification content (customer name and follow-up description) is generated on your device from your call report data and is never transmitted externally as part of the notification itself.

Push Notifications: The app requests permission to send push notifications. If you grant permission, your device token is transmitted to and stored on WP Field's servers. Notifications are used to send follow-up reminders for scheduled customer visits and to deliver administrative messages from your sales management team (such as announcements and time-sensitive communications). They are not used for marketing. You can disable notifications at any time in iOS Settings.

The device token itself is not personally identifiable but is associated with your account. It is used only to deliver in-app notifications and is not shared with third parties.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account from within the app by going to Settings and selecting Delete Account. Deleting your account removes your personal identifying information (name and email address) from our servers. Call report content you have submitted is retained as a business record per Wear Parts & Equipment Co.'s internal data retention policy. To request manual data deletion or for any data-related inquiry, contact us at the address below.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your employment relationship with Wear Parts & Equipment Co. continues, or as needed for business record-keeping purposes.
